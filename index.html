About Dentina.Ai

Made by a dentist for dentists

Dentina.Ai brings to life an AI dental receptionist, streamlining the process of scheduling visits with dentists and hygienists. Our solution ensures efficient and accessible appointment bookings, tailored to each patient's needs.

By automating administrative tasks, Dentina.Ai enhances the coordination of dental care, allowing practices to focus on delivering exceptional services. Our platform is a leap towards improved patient experience and operational efficiency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Currently, Dentina integrates with Dentrix, Dentrix Enterprise, Eaglesoft, Opendental, PractiWorks, OrthoTrac, Dolphin, Dentrix Ascend, eClinicalWorks, Athena, Curve, Cloud9, and Denticon. No affiliation or endorsement implied.

Phone integration and/or forwarding is simple! Dentina works seamlessly with major VOIP (i.e. Mango, Weave) and telephone providers (i.e. Spectrum, AT&T), including any phone system that allows call forwarding.

Dentina answers calls after hours (or 24/7!), schedules appointments, sends texts, responds to general business inquiries, and updates you and/or your staff after every interaction via email or text. Dentina is multi-lingual, fully customizable, and integrates with the most popular PMS systems. Each day Dentina is becoming more advanced with new features and functionality.

While the standard plans are intended for after-hours and overflow, Dentina can answer all of your calls and even transfer calls to your staff when desired. There is an extra cost for this feature. Please book a demo to learn more.

Dentina employs robust security measures to ensure full HIPAA compliance, safeguarding patient data with end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and secure cloud-based storage solutions. Our commitment to privacy and security is designed to give dental practices peace of mind regarding their patient information.
